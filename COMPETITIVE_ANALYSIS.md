# Competitive Analysis: ChartPlugin.com vs Find&Tell Charts

**Date:** 2025
**Competitor:** https://chartplugin.com
**Our Product:** https://charts.findandtell.co

---

## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** ChartPlugin and Find&Tell Charts are **NOT direct competitors** - they serve fundamentally different markets and use cases.

- **ChartPlugin:** Figma/Sketch plugin for designers creating mockups (413K+ users, $10/year)
- **Find&Tell:** Web-based standalone chart builder for business users ($9/mo planned)

**Strategic Recommendation:** Position Find&Tell as "The Funnel Chart Specialist for Business Teams" - focusing on interactive embeds, business use cases, and platform independence.

## Conclusion

**The Opportunity:** Become the "Figma for Business Charts" - specializing in funnel visualizations with interactive embeds that work everywhere.

**The Strategy:** Focus on what ChartPlugin CAN'T do (web embeds, PowerPoint, business workflows) rather than competing on what they do well (designer integration, chart quantity).

**The Market:** Large enough for both products to succeed in their respective niches. ChartPlugin = designers. Find&Tell = business teams.
